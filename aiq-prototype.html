Content Matrix

AI agents scan
every page.
Every day.

After each AIQ generation, and daily across all existing content, AI agents automatically classify every page into one of four categories, each with a specific prescribed action.

● Prime

High traffic + high conversion. Top-performing content.

→ Atomize and repurpose

● Promising

High conversion, low traffic. Untapped potential.

→ Amplify distribution

● Concerning

High traffic, low conversion. Engagement without results.

→ Optimize with AIQ

● Corrosive

Low traffic + low conversion. Draining resources.

→ Rewrite or remove
